    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: How do register a Campaign?
    A: Go here or manually go to the tab Campaigns in the navigation bar and click on Add Campaign.
    Q: How do register a Linksite?
    A: Go here or manually go to the tab Linksites in the navigation bar and click on Add Linksite.
    Q: Will the Campaigns/Linksites be available for all of the other customers?
    A: No, all the Campaigns and Linksites will only be available to you and your assigned users.
    Q: How do I add a user?
    A: If you're a superuser, go to the Admin page and click Add User. If you're not a superuser, please contact your Employer/SuperUser.
    Q: How do I see if I'm a SuperUser?
    A: Go to the homepage, there will be a line which says: Status: User or Status: Admin. If it says Admin you're a SuperUser.
